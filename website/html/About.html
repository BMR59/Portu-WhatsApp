About

This project is an ongoing process being worked on by students at the University of Pittsburgh under the supervision and instruction of Dr. Greg Bondar in a Computational Methods in the Humanities course. This project in particular is analyzing data gathered from L1 Portuguese speakers from Brazil and L2 Portuguese speakers from the University of Pittsburgh. The objective of this project is to observe cultural/linguistic phenomenona based in texting convetions (grammatical/cultural errors, pauses, puncuation charactersitics, emjois) and the use of (un)corrected language.
